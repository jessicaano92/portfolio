My Biography

I love the outdoors, biking, camping, my friends and family. Nature is my therapy! I am a total crime junkie and fan girl over crime podcasts and movies. Talk coffee and sushi to me and I will be your best friend! I'm a seasoned traveler and love exploring new countries and trying new cuisines. I've been a flight attendant for the past 4 years and it has been an amazing experience. I've traveled almost all over the United States and internationally including Europe, Japan, South America, and Canada. I have been married to the love of my life for 7 years and we have an awesome chocolate lab named Oakley. We are originally from Southern Oregon and have lived in 5 different states but currently reside in Colorado!
